# myWardrobe
### A simple, quick, modern webapp to catalogue and organize digitally your clothes

## Backstory
Pretty basic: I needed a single place to quickly and easily get my clothes organized, to 
understand what replacements I needed, what was missing, and what I could get rid of.

Did some research online and on the store but didn't find anything suitable, so I just thought
"you know what, let me make something myself". This is the result.
