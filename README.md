# myWardrobe
### A simple and quick webapp to cataloge and digitally organize your clothes
> #### Visit it on: https://my-wardrobe-app.vercel.app/

## Backstory
I randomly needed a single place to quickly and easily get my clothes organized, to 
understand what replacements I needed, what was missing, and what I could get rid of. When i searched online
for some service I didn't really find anything suitable or free AND good enough, so I just thought
"you know what, let me make something myself".
